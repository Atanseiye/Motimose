Welcome to
Motimosé Metals

Motimosé Metals is a multi-disciplinary geo-mining company with an eclectic mix of vastly experienced Qualified Professionals that has been structured to provide detailed and comprehensive mining industry expertise based on a cumulative of 300 years’ experience and skill to the develop mining assets through their various stages of development.

Combining frontline technical knowhow and practical experience to ensure that mining projects across various mineral ores progress from pre-exploration to production with a focus on creating and delivering value to our clients.

At Motimosé, all our products and services are designed specifically to suit the client, mineral, location, and project peculiarities in line with global best practice on the environment and health, safety and security.

We are able to employ the in-house geoscientists, production engineers & project finance teams experience to provide full spectrum analysis to tackle a range of project complexities from small and simple asset reconnaissance work to large multi-mineral production and processing facilities across planning, design and implementation, to final interpretation, reporting and analysis.
